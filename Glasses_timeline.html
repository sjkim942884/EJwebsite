Introduction/Overview

According to experts, glasses are the fifth most important invention sice humans discovered fire and invented wheels. Because millions of people with bad eyesight can finally see well. Today, we take it for granted to wear glasses, but in the past, people who suffered from vision loss had to look forward to the invention of glasses. It took a long time for the modern glasses to be developed. This process required a number of experiments, and various forms of glasses were created and disappeared. It was a very slow development compared to the recognition that glasses were important.

Project Goal

It explains the parts and history of glasses to users.
(1) User can find which parts of their eye glasses are broken,
(2) find the better eye glasses,
(3) learn history of eye glasses.
