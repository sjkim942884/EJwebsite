Overview

As things become digital, things such as driver's license, credit cards and business cards that should be in personal wallets are now in smartphones. But not everyone uses it. Many people still prefer physical driver's licenses. How can we make digital driver's licenses more convenient for people to use and like it?

Objectives

•To translate information across different from factors through core visual design principles
•To understand the advantages and disadvantages of specific graphical forms of communication
•To present typographic information in an effective
•To identify and leverage appropriate information structures and interactions to best utilize the identified device environment
